Greetings! My name is Jeff Abbott. I first found the passion for music through percussion, or the drums. Feeling inspired by musicians that played multiple instruments, I decided to learn to play the guitar. Not long after that I started to play the bass. The understanding of music theory helped transition across all instruments. Join the band of music and learn an instrument today!
